Stake imports require the native XLSX export from stake.com.au (not CSV).

To download yours:
  Stake → Portfolio → Activity → Export → XLSX

The importer reads two sheets by name:
  - "Aus Equities"       (all AUD-denominated)
  - "Wall St Equities"   (USD prices, with USD/AUD rate column)

Both sheets share this column order (row 1 is headers):
  A  Trade Date              (YYYY-MM-DD)
  B  Settlement Date
  C  Symbol                  (e.g. VAS, AAPL)
  D  Name
  E  Side                    ("Buy" or "Sell")
  F  Trade Identifier
  G  Units
  H  Avg. Price
  I  Value
  J  Fees
  K  GST
  L  Total Value
  M  Currency
  N  AUD/USD rate            (Wall St sheet only; USD→AUD multiplier, e.g. 1.52)

Only Buy/Sell rows are imported. Deposits, dividends, and interest are ignored.

If your export is missing sheet names, rename them exactly as above before uploading.
